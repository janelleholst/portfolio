About

Hi, I'm Janelle!

I am currently in my senior year at Arizona State University in the Graphic Information Technology program with a primary focus in user centered design and a secondary focus in front end development. Professionally, I am interesting in getting behind the ‘why’ of people’s decisions so as to improve my users’ experiences. I have had experience creating wireframes, mock ups, prototypes, user flows, personas, heuristic evaluations, user testing and I am excited to get my hands on more. I’ve worked in Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD, Sketch, and Figma.

My personal interests include traveling, encountering new experiences, being outdoors, meeting new people, and learning new things. I am determined to make my passions my reality and to get out of my comfort zone to increase my knowledge and abilities. I’m ready to apply my work ethic in an environment where I can learn from those around me, grow from my failures, and have fun with it!
